The Best Tuna Melt

This melt will make you want to melt!

For many, Tuna Melts don't come to mind first for dinner. In my family the tuna melt was a comfort food. The right amount of spices and mayonnaise and you'll find yourself among the clouds in heaven. If you are really into cheese pulls then this is the sandwich for you. Get ready to cozy up on the couch, enjoy your homemade goodness and watch your favorite show on a rainy day.

For most recipes, you need to follow many directions that involve pots, pans, ovens and microwaves. Well here you're only going to need a frying pan, a can of tuna, mayonnaise, your favorite spices, and white or wheat bread. Trust me, dinner at home never sounded so good. Enjoy!

Ingredients you will need

  • 2 slices of white or wheat bread
  • 1 can of tuna in water
  • 3 tbsp of mayonnaise and extra for frying
  • Butter as needed
  • 1 tbsp of mustard
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of pepper
  • 1/2 tsp of paprika
  • 1/6 of a yellow onion
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • A slice or two of your favorite cheese. Below are my favorites.
    • American
    • Cheddar
    • Swiss
    • Gruyere
    • Pepper Jack

Directions:

  1. Open your can of tuna leaving a small hinge so that it is still attached to the rest of the can. Squeeze out as much water as you can. Then use a fork or knife to pry the lid open again.
  2. Mix the mayonnaise, mustard, onion, garlic and spices together.
  3. The pour in your tuna. Mix well. Make sure it is to your taste.
  4. Put butter on one side of two slices of bread.
  5. Put your tuna mixture on one side of the bread. Lay a slice or two of your preferred cheese on top. Put the other side of the bread on top with the butter side facing inwards.
  6. Turn on the burner under your frying pan.
  7. While the pan heats, spread mayonnaise on the top of the sandwich. Be generous and cover evenly.
  8. Once the pan is hot, use a spatula to carefully flip the sandwich into the pan on the side with the mayonnaise.
  9. Let the bottom of the sandwich cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown on the bottom. While it cooks put mayonnaise on the now top of the sandwich. Once the underside is golden and crispy, flip the sandwich over so the other side can cook. Fry until golden.
  10. Once you have a golden sandwich and the cheese appears to be melted, remove the sandwich from the pan. Put on a plate. Slice diagonally. Serve immediately with Lays or Doritos chips. Enjoy!
