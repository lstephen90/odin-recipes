The Best Egg Salad

Egg Salad can be a dream!

Some people don't think you can liven up an egg salad but I'm here to tell you they are wrong. First, we must start with the basics. Everyone knows an egg salad needs mayonnaise. There's no doubt about it. Then mustard. Next, things really get spicy with the spices!! This is one of the most special parts of the recipe. I like to add salt, pepper, paprika, dill, garlic powder, and for a hint of home, some curry powder. Spices aren't the only special parts of this recipe.

To take your egg salad to the next level, you'll want to diced onion into the egg mixture. Yellow or green onion are my preferences. Then serve the salad on a bed of spinach on wheat toast. To top it all off, slice grape tomatoes and face them down on the egg. Now, go ENJOY!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 4-6 grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup spanich (choose this based on how large your wheat toast is)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1-1/2 tbsp of mustard
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp pepper
  • 1tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp dill
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 or more curry powder
  • 1/5 onion chopped/1 green onion

Directions:

  1. Boil 2 eggs for 10-15 minutes depending on your stove top. Burners 10 minutes and Electric 15 minutes.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, mustard and spices.Put in fridge to keep chill.
  3. Chop 1/5 or more onion to your taste.
  4. After hard boil time is over, pour out hot water. Then dump hot eggs into and ice bath. Wait 10-15 minutes.
  5. Remove the shell from the boiled egg.Get mixture and place deshelled eggs on top.
  6. Take 2 pieces of wheat bread and toast to your liking.
  7. Mash hard boiled eggs into the mixture until smooth. Small chunks are okay.
  8. Stir in chopped onion.
  9. Once toast is complete, lay half of your spinach on each piece of toast.
  10. Distribute your egg salad evenly between the toast.
  11. Then place sliced grape tomatoes face down on the salad.
  12. Breakfast, lunch, or dinner is served! Enjoy!
